### KL散度

$$
\begin{align*}
KL(p || q) =&& \int_x p(x) \log \frac{p(x)}{q(x)} dx \\
=&& E_{x\sim p(x)}[\log \frac{p(x)}{q(x)}]
\end{align*}
$$
